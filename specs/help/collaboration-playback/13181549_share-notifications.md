# Share Notifications

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/13181549-share-notifications
> Category: collaboration-playback

---

When receiving notifications via share links, the notification settings differ depending on whether their Frame.io access is as a Reviewer or as a Member. 
​## If the user is a ReviewerReviewers will receive notification updates via digest emails on a 15 minute digest basis. They must comment on a shared asset to receive notifications from that asset. 

 

Email notifications will be sent out to Reviewers when:

Invited to a new share link

Comment created*

Reply created*

@ mentioned created*

** only in the assets that they’ve comment on within the share link*
Reviewers can also turn off a share link&#x27;s notifications in the Share or directly from the email.
​ 

## If the user is a Frame.io memberAdvanced notification settings are available to members of an account. Navigate to **Settings &gt; Notifications** to configure the frequency and triggers. Notifications can be enabled/disabled on a per Project or per Workspace basis.
