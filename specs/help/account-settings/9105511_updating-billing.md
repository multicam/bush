# Updating your billing information

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105511-updating-your-billing-information
> Category: account-settings

---

## Update Payment and Billing InformationPayment and billing information can be added or updated on the [Billing page](https://next.frame.io/settings/billing) (Account Owner permissions required). 
​Select **Add Card** under the Payment Information to add or update a card.
​ 

## Invoice informationA company name and tax ID can be added or updated on the [Billing page](https://next.frame.io/settings/billing) (Account Owner permissions required).
Select **Edit** under Invoice Information to update your invoice details.
