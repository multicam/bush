# Switching between accounts

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105512-switching-between-accounts
> Category: account-settings

---

When you are added to multiple accounts on Frame.io, you will have the option to Switch between accounts to view a desired Workspace or Project you are added to.
​
Navigate to your account logo in the bottom left hand corner to switch to another account you are added to.
