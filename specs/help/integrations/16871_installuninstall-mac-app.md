# Installing and uninstalling the Frame.io Mac App

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/16871-installing-and-uninstalling-the-frame-io-mac-app
> Category: integrations

---

**Installing the Frame.io Mac App**Installing the Frame.io Mac App is simple. Go to your App Store and then [search for &quot;Frame.io.&quot;](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/frame-io-video-collaboration/id992958444?mt=12) 

If you want to read more about the updates, click on **Frame.io**. Otherwise, click **GET**. 

 

After downloading the app, sign in to your account, and you can start using the Frame.io Mac App.

 

 

**Uninstalling the Mac App**If you would like to uninstall the Frame.io Mac App, open Applications, locate the Frame.io Mac App, and then drag it into the Trash Bin.

 

 

*Note**: This will not adjust your Frame.io account.
