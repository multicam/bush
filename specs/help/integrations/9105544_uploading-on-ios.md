# Uploading files on the iOS app

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105544-uploading-files-on-the-ios-app
> Category: integrations

---

Upload media directly from Photos or Files into a project. 
​ 
Tap the three dots on the top right corner of your project, tap &quot;Upload,&quot; and then choose an upload source. 
​ 

Upload multiple files at a time by selecting them and then choosing &quot;Done&quot; on the top right.
