# Image Viewer on iOS

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105562-image-viewer-on-ios
> Category: integrations

---

Frame.io can render images up to 8K in resolution and supports high-res images in over 100 RAW image formats, including Fujifilm, Canon, Nikon, Panasonic, and Sony in-app, powered by Adobe Camera RAW.

 

​**Zoom** on an image by pinching with your fingers. 
​
Double-tap to **view at 100%**, double-tap again to return your view to &quot;fit to screen.&quot; 
​
Toggle the pinned comments view by tapping the **Gear** icon at the bottom of the page.
