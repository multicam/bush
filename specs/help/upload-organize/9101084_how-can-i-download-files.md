# How can I download files?

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9101084-how-can-i-download-files
> Category: upload-organize

---

There are a few different ways to download assets within Frame.io.
​**Download All Clips in a Folder**To download an entire folder, you can click the three-dot menu on the folder and select “Download” from the drop-down menu. 
​**Download Individual Clips**To download a single file, select the three-dot menu on a clip &gt; Download &gt; download the original file or a smaller proxy file.
​
Downloading from within the player page is also an option. Select the file name at the top of the player page and select “Download“
​
To download all assets within the folder you are in, select “Download All Assets” from the top of the Project page.
​**Download Bulk Clips**If you are looking to download two or more items at once you can press Shift and select bulk select the files. Command/Control on your keyboard will add and select multiple files/folders individually. 

 

## Download with Transfer 

When you click the Download button in Frame.io, you&#x27;ll be asked to choose whether to **Download in Browser **or **Download with App**, referring to the **Frame.io Transfer** app. If you have the Transfer app, your download will immediately be sent to it to begin single or bulk downloading. Click **Always use this download method **to save your preferred download setting.
