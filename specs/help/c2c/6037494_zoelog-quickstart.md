# C2C: ZoeLog QuickStart Guide

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/6037494-c2c-zoelog-quickstart-guide
> Category: c2c

---

[ZoeLog](https://zoelog.io/) is a powerful camera report app for iOS devices. With Frame.io C2C, you can upload your camera reports directly into your Project alongside your video and audio C2C assets. 

Use this guide to quickly setup ZoeLog for C2C with settings recommended by Frame.io.

 

**What you&#x27;ll need**A **paid** subscription to ZoeLogZoeLog app installed on iPhone or iPad

A ZoeLog [Production](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UJbvgzXF3AY) with users addedA connection to the [internet](https://blog.frame.io/2021/03/15/connecting-your-set-to-the-internet/) (WiFi or cellular)A Frame.io project

*Specific to V3 accounts:**Ensure C2C Connections are enabled in your project settings**Ensure you have C2C Device management permissions within the account the project is hosted in*
 

**Setup**In your working Production, go to the **Share** menu (this is at the bottom of the list of camera sidebar).In the Share menu, scroll to the bottom to find the Frame.io section.

Log in using your Frame.io credentials and click **Allow** when prompted to let ZoeLog upload into Frame.io.After you&#x27;ve logged in, tap **Connect a project** and navigate to the Project you want to upload reports to.Once you&#x27;re connected to a Project, make sure the Frame.io toggle is enabled.

Use the rest of the options in the Share menu to configure your camera report and choose what file types to upload.

Once you&#x27;re ready to share, hit the **Send** button to upload your reports to Frame.io.
