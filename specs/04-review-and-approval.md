# Bush - Review & Approval

## Summary
Bush's review and approval system reduces review cycles across all asset types with powerful, centralized feedback and review capabilities. Per the Pfeiffer Report, teams experience **2.7x faster review and approval** across stakeholders.

## Precision Feedback

### Anchored Comments
- Click anywhere to leave precise, accurate feedback
- Comments anchor to specific locations on assets

### Comment Attachments
- Attach references with feedback for added context
- Support for images, videos, or documents

### Enhanced Comment Features
- Emojis and reactions
- Comment links
- Streamlined discussion
