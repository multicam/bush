# Frame.io - Workflow Management

## Summary
Frame.io's workflow management enables teams to connect people, assets, actions, and communications on a powerful, flexible, secure cloud platform for faster and more effective work.

## Metadata & Collections

### Flexible Metadata System
- **33 built-in metadata fields**
- Custom metadata fields support
- Field types include: status, selects, rating, assignee, keywords, due date

### Collections Feature
- Sort and group assets into unique Collections
- Collections act as flexible building blocks
- Real-time updates when assets change
- Share once, sync everywhere - no multiple links

### Use Cases
1. **Social Deliverables**: Organize thousands of deliverables by platform, duration, release date
2. **Casting Selects**: Categorize auditions by role, selects, etc., filter and group into Collections
3. **Team-wide Collections**: "Approved Assets" or "Assets by Assignee" for team coordination

## Custom Workflow Building

### Design Custom Structures
- Collaborate with peace of mind knowing content is protected by industry-leading security

### Workflow Patterns
- Design custom structures per project/team
- Group assets by assignee, due date, status
- Tag incoming assets with metadata
- Automatic syncing across Collections

## Automatic Syncing
- Collections update in real time
- Share once and sync everywhere
- No more crossed wires or multiple links
- Eliminate version confusion
