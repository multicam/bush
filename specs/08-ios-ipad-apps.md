# Bush - iOS & iPad Apps

Phase 2 Feature.

## Summary
Built natively in Swift, the award-winning Bush iOS and iPadOS apps bring all the tools teams need for untethered creative management.

## App Features

### Review and Approval (Mobile)
- Advanced review and approval on mobile screen
- Customize view to maximize productivity
- Adjustable thumbnail size (small to see more assets)
- Select visible metadata for decision-making
- Fast filtering and sorting

### High-Performance Viewing
- Pinch to zoom up to 400%
- Double-tap to jump
- Swipe to navigate
- Customizable skip interval
- Adjustable playback speed
- Resolution control
- Fluid transition from viewing to commenting
- Every action is precise and intuitive

### Sharing from Mobile
- Create and send custom Shares from iPhone/iPad
- Choose background image and accent colors
- Add logo and descriptions
- Configure viewer style (reels or grid)
- Control visibility and permissions
- All settings at your fingertips

### Management Features
- Manage files, projects, teams
- Communications management
- Notification controls
- Quick access to users, files, projects, permissions
- Filter notifications to reduce noise

## Apple TV App

### Bush for Apple TV
- View Project's videos, images, and audio
- Big screen presentation
- High-resolution 4K 10-bit HDR viewing
- Customizable resolution controls
- Adjustable playback speed

## Technical Details
- Native Swift development
- Award-winning app design
- Optimized for iOS and iPadOS
