# Bush - Integrations

## Summary
Bush integrates with leading creative tools to keep workflows seamless and creatives productive.

## Adobe Integrations

### Adobe Premiere Pro
- Seamless collaboration within Premiere
- Frame-accurate notes right in editor's workspace
- Comments appear as markers
- Keep creatives in the zone without context switching

### Adobe Lightroom
- Upload photos directly from location
- Direct Bush to Lightroom connection
- Rapid content creation workflow

## Apple Integrations

### Final Cut Pro
- Frame-accurate feedback as markers on timeline
- Direct integration with editing workflow
- Editor sees Bush comments in FCP

### iOS/iPadOS
- Native Swift apps
- Full feature parity for mobile review

### Apple TV
- 4K 10-bit HDR viewing
- Big screen presentations

## Software Integrations
- Professional NLE support
- Creative suite compatibility

## Hardware Integrations
