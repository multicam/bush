# Frame.io - Transcription & Captions

## Summary
Frame.io provides AI-powered transcription and caption capabilities to make video content searchable and accessible.

## Features

### Transcription
- Generate searchable transcripts automatically
- AI-powered speech-to-text
- Automatic speaker identification
- Searchable across all transcribed content

### Captions
- Share videos with captions
- Auto-generated from transcripts
- Support for accessibility compliance
- Searchable captions

## Use Cases

### Content Search
- Find specific moments in video by searching transcript text
- Locate speaker mentions
- Navigate by spoken content

### Accessibility
- Captions for hearing-impaired viewers
- Compliance with accessibility standards
