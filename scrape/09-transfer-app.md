# Frame.io - Transfer App

## Summary
The Frame.io Transfer app enables quick upload and download of large files or entire projects with a single click. It provides fast, efficient, and reliable bulk transfers.

## Download Options
- **macOS (Apple Silicon)**: DMG installer
- **macOS (Intel)**: DMG installer
- **Windows**: EXE installer

## Key Features

### Speed & Reliability
- Move bulk content at high speeds
- Rivals fastest transfer services
- Reliable transfer completion

### Organized Transfers
- Folder structures remain intact
- Easier media and project management
- No reorganization needed after transfer

### User Control
- Control transfer speeds
- Drag-and-drop to prioritize uploads
- Simple, intuitive interface

### Cross-Platform
- Windows support
- Mac support (Intel and Apple Silicon)

## Use Cases
- Large video files
- Complete project folders
- Bulk asset migration
- Archive downloads
