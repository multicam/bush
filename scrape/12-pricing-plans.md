# Frame.io - Pricing Plans

## Overview
Frame.io offers four tiers: Free, Pro, Team, and Enterprise. Monthly and annual billing available (13% discount for annual).

## Plan Comparison

### Free - $0/month
**For basic use**

- Up to 2 members
- 2GB storage
- Up to 2 projects
- Camera to Cloud
- Transcription and captions
- SOC Type 2 compliant
- TPN+ Gold Shield accredited
- Social Sign-On
- Frame.io iOS app
- Frame.io tvOS App
- Frame.io Transfer app
- Premiere Pro integration
- Frame.io to Lightroom connection
- Knowledge base
- Email support
- Adobe Support - Standard

### Pro - $15/member/month + tax
**For professionals**

Everything in Free, plus:
- Up to 5 members
- 2 TB included + 2TB per additional member
- Unlimited projects
- Custom-branded shares
- Passphrase-protected shares
- Share link expiration
- Share permissions (disable downloads/comments, hide versions)
- Comment attachments
- One static watermark template
- In-app chat support

### Team - $25/member/month + tax
**Most popular - For teams**

Everything in Pro, plus:
- Up to 15 members
- 3TB included + 2TB per additional member
- Restricted projects
- Restricted folders
- Internal comments (separate from client conversations)

### Enterprise - Custom Pricing
**For multiple teams**

Everything in Team, plus:
- Custom members and storage
- Single sign-on (SAML2-based SSO)
- Multiple team workspaces
- Session-based watermarking (Enterprise Prime)
- Unlimited watermarking templates
- Asset Lifecycle Management
- Secure Sharing (Enterprise Prime)
- Priority in-app support (24/5, one-hour response)
- Storage Connect (Enterprise Prime)
- Forensic Watermarking (Enterprise Prime)
- Digital Rights Management (Enterprise Prime)
- Payment by invoice
- Custom invoicing period
- Dedicated account manager
- Enterprise SLA
- Custom trainings

## Enterprise Tiers
- **Enterprise Select**: Standard Enterprise
- **Enterprise Prime**: Includes Storage Connect, Secure Sharing, Session-Based Watermarking, Forensic Watermarking, DRM

## Billing
- Monthly or Annual (13% annual discount)
- Auto-payment via card (all except Enterprise)
- Payment by invoice (Enterprise only)
- Sales tax applied based on billing address

## Free Trial
- Enter contact and billing info to start
- No charge during trial period
- Auto-enrollment at trial end
- Cancel anytime before trial ends
