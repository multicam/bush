# Watch Frame.io with the Apple TV App

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/6084818-watch-frame-io-with-the-apple-tv-app
> Category: integrations

---

Frame.io is now available as an Apple TV App! Take your viewing experience from desktop and mobile onto your TV for all your Frame.io assets with 4K HDR-10 bit playback support.

 

**The Frame.io Apple TV App highlights include:**Simple navigation through your Workspaces and Projects 

High-resolution 10-bit 4K HDR playback 

Adjustable quality playback settings and speed control

Easy autoplay through an entire folder of assets or jump between clips using the Play Queue

Supports still photos and audio files 

 

# Watch the Walkthrough **Getting Started**Once you download the app from the Apple TV App Store, set up your Frame.io account via QR Code activation or by going to [activate.frame.io](http://activate.frame.io/) and entering your 6 digit code. This will create your user profile in the TV App. Up to 10 users can be added as saved profiles. 

To read more about how to activate your account, click [here](https://help.frame.io/en/articles/6125946-authenticating-the-frame-io-apple-tv-app). 

# Navigating through the Apple TV App### Workspaces and Projects (V4 Experience)The Frame.io Apple TV App, when used with your Frame.io V4 account, will navigate through your Workspaces and Projects, similar to the web experience of Frame.io. 

 

 

Once logged in, you will have access to all Workspaces and Projects that are available to you. Selecting the Account name will show you all the accounts you can access in the app. Selecting your User name will give you options to Switch User, Reset Passcode, and Sign Out.

 

 

Browse through any Workspaces available to you, or select a recent Project to jump directly into. Inside the projects, you&#x27;ll see the organized folders and assets with file names, upload date, length, and version stack shown on the thumbnail. Select an asset to open up the player page.

 

**Inbox (Legacy Experience)** 

The Frame.io Apple TV App, when used with your Frame.io Legacy account, will host all review and presentation links inside of your Inbox for playback. The Inbox feature works for all Team Members, Collaborators, and Secure Reviewers in your account. Sending any Review or Presentation Link via Invite Only enabled or as a direct email share will deliver to their inbox.

 

Once logged in, you will have access to all inboxes across all accounts that are available to you. Selecting the Account name will show you all the accounts you can access in the app. Selecting your User name will give you options to Switch User, Reset Passcode, and Sign Out.

 

Highlighting each listing will show a preview image, created by, created at, file size, and time received, along with an optional description of the shared content. Then select the asset you want to view or select **Play All** to start a Play Queue for all the assets in the shared link. 

**Playback** 

When viewing your assets, playback can be controlled through the Apple TV remote for pausing and playing. Clicking left and right will skip the video backward and forward 10 seconds. Navigating to the player bar will bring up options to choose When Playback Ends, Enable Captions, View Audio Track, and Enable Picture-in-picture Mode.

 

 

Swiping or clicking down will pull up the Play Queue. Navigate here to see other assets and folders inside of the share link. Click on one to skip to that asset or you can let it autoplay in the queue.

 

 

Playback speed and picture quality can also be adjusted during playback. Select the cog icon above the player bar to adjust both options.

 

 

 

**FAQ****Q: Can I make comments while viewing any asset on the Frame.io Apple TV App?****A: **At this time, the functionality to leave comments is not available through the Frame.io Apple TV App. We hope to bring the feature to the app through future updates. 

**Q: Is the Apple TV app a free download?** A: Yes, The Frame.io app can be downloaded from the Apple TV app store for free. 

 

**Q: What’s the difference between Apple TV, tvOS, and Apple TV+?** A: Apple TV is a hardware box much like Roku or Amazon Firestick. It has an operating system, similar to how the iPhone uses iOS. The operating system from Apple TV is called tvOS. Sometimes the terms are used interchangeably. To further complicate things, Apple also has a streaming service called AppleTV+ that runs on other devices and smart TVs. The frame.io Apple TV app runs exclusively on Apple TV hardware. 

 

**Q: Does my Apple ID for the app store need to be the as my Frame.io  email?** A: No. Your Apple ID email does not need to be the same email you use to sign in to Frame.io on Apple TV. 

 

**Q: What are the hardware system requirements for the Frame.io AppleTV app?** A: Here are the requirements for the frame.io Apple TV app: 

Apple TV minimum OS requirement: tvOS 17 

Apple TV minimum hardware requirement: Apple TV HD or 4K
