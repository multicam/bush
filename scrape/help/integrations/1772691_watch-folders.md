# Creating Watch Folders with the Frame.io Mac App

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/1772691-creating-watch-folders-with-the-frame-io-mac-app
> Category: integrations

---

Connect a local folder to a project in Frame.io for a faster and easier way to upload your files. When selecting the Frame.io icon in the menu bar, select the &quot;Watch Folders&quot; tab on the right. 

Next, click **Add+** and choose a local folder from your computer to watch. Everything in this selected folder will be uploaded to your project in Frame.io without needing to upload it manually. It works for you.
