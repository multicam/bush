# Multipage PDF viewer

> Source: https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105330-multipage-pdf-viewer
> Category: collaboration-playback

---

Upload your multipage PDF files into Frame.io, and they will be rendered in high resolution.

Use the **up/down** arrow keys to go between pagesScroll down the page drawer on the left side, or jump to a specific part of the PDF by typing and entering your desired page number at the bottom of the left panel.

**Loupe** by holding **Shift** to inspect things at 100% resolution.**Zoom in and out** by pressing the **+/-** buttons, then use **Space + drag** to pan through your document
 

**Commenting on your PDF File**Selecting a page from the left panel will allow you to leave comments on specific pages, including anchored comments, making feedback and page markup a more organized process for teams.
​
If you need to leave a comment that’s not specific to a page, clicking on the book icon will post it across the entire PDF. 

 

# FAQ**Q: Is there a size or page limit when viewing PDF files?**
​**A:** For the best viewing experience, we do not recommend uploading files larger than 1000 pages or 1 GB in size.
